On the Subject of The Weakest Link

Your flavor text should go here.

You are a contestant on the game show “Weakest Link”. You must answer tough trivia questions and eliminate fellow contestants in order to win the competition.

Skill Check

Keep track of the other two contestants’ names and skills. Find what your skill is based on the day the bomb was generated. Press the logo to start the clock.

Question Phase

The three of you will have 2 minutes to correctly answer as many trivia questions as possible. Keep track of the ratio of the number of questions answered correctly out of how many questions were asked* to that person for each contestant. Not answering at least 3 questions or not answering over 50% correctly will cause you to be voted as the weakest link, which will lead to a strike. Use the keyboard to type in your answers.

*If the timer goes off before the question is answered, it does not count as a question that was asked

Elimination Phase

You will now need to choose who you will vote as the weakest link. If only one contestant has the same skill as you, type in their name to eliminate them.

Otherwise, use the two decimal points (rounded down) from the ratio taken from the question phase as your base. For each character in the contestant's name, if the character is in the serial number, add 1 to the percentage. Convert numbers into letters (1=A, 2=B, etc) with 0 meaning J. Eliminate the person who has the lowest percentage.

If there is a tie, eliminate the person whose name is alphabetically first.

Money Phase

You will have to cooperate with the remaining contestant to hit the big prize of $1000. You will have 3 minutes for the both of you to correctly answer questions consecutively. Use the chart below in order to figure out which type of question will come up next.

If the type of question doesn’t match your contestant’s skill, press “bank” to cash in your money. Not doing so will likely cause the other contestant to answer incorrectly, breaking the chain and losing all non-banked money.

The round will end if:

  1. You both correctly answered 9 questions consecutively.
  2. You have over $1000 in the bank.
  3. Time runs out. If less than $1000 are in the bank, a strike will occur.

Face Off

You will now have to face the other contestant in order to win the “Weakest Link”. Get 3 out 5 questions right in order to win. Not doing so will have you lose the competition, causing a strike.
